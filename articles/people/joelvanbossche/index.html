Joel van Bossche
Joel van Bossche, born on September 3rd, 2008, is an Austrian runner, theatre actor, and dedicated student whose life already reflects an impressive blend of discipline, creativity, and ambition.

Growing up in Austria, he developed an early fascination for both the precision of athletics and the expressive world of performing arts — two passions that continue to shape his identity today.

Now 17 years old, Joel lives in Graz, the cultural heart of Styria. The city’s mix of historical charm and youthful energy provides the perfect environment for his personal and artistic development. Whether he is training on the track, rehearsing for a performance, or exploring new ideas with friends, Graz has become the place where his talents continue to grow.

Alongside his athletic and artistic pursuits, Joel studies at the Benko Business School, where he strengthens his academic skills and prepares for future opportunities. His education not only deepens his understanding of business and economics but also complements his broader ambitions.

Balancing sports, theatre, and academics, Joel embodies the spirit of a young individual determined to explore his full potential. With a growing list of experiences and a clear drive to excel, he is steadily building a multifaceted profile that reflects both his versatility and his dedication to personal growth.
